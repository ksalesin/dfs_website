about

Interested in fashion, technology, and the living systems that connect them? We're seeking artists, designers, and makers to submit to our Spring 2026 exhibition! Your work could appear in a runway show and exhibition at the Hopkins Center for the Arts (HOP) in June 2026. Theme: "Entangled Ecologies." Open to interpretation, exploring the entanglement of bodies, environments, and machines. Think: fabric meets code, textiles become electronic, and technology mirrors ecology.
